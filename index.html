One place to report issues affecting AAUA students and indigenes

IRO connects Akungba, Iwaro, Etioro and Ayegunle communities with a simple way to raise, track, and resolve local issues — from campus concerns to community infrastructure.

Report an issue View reported issues

Who this is for

Students

Raise concerns about hostels, campus facilities, security, or academic services and follow up on their status.

Indigenes

Report community issues such as roads, water, electricity, or safety in Akungba, Iwaro, Etioro and Ayegunle.

Administrators

Review incoming reports, see what matters most to the community, and mark issues as resolved.
